# Design DNA — Floreth

## Visual Personality: organic-natural
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary: #C97B5A → `oklch(0.62 0.10 42)` — deep apricot/terracotta
- Accent: #F2C97E → `oklch(0.85 0.10 75)` — buttery golden yellow
- Sage: #8EA98A → `oklch(0.68 0.05 148)` — botanical sage green
- Surface: #FAF3EC → `oklch(0.97 0.015 75)` — warm cream/apricot background
- Text Dark: `oklch(0.25 0.025 42)` — warm near-black (replaces surface-900)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weights 300/400/500/600/700 (dramatic serif headlines)
- Body: Montserrat — weights 300/400/500/600 (clean humanist sans-serif)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Pressed botanical linen texture** — subtle noise overlay + SVG organic dividers between sections give a "watercolor paper / linen stationery" feel
2. **Dramatic scale contrast** — hero headline at text-7xl/text-8xl Cormorant Garamond 300 weight with wide letter-spacing; body text stays at 18px Montserrat 300-400
3. **Asymmetric storytelling rhythm** — alternating left/right text-image columns, staggered card offsets, no two sections share the same layout structure
4. **Warm apricot morning-light hero** — radial glows from terracotta and gold at low chroma with grain overlay; no purple/blue anywhere
5. **Rounded botanical card language** — 24px+ border-radius on all cards, warm-tinted shadows, pill CTAs — every element feels soft and handcrafted
